blog 5- my trip to London.

10th august, 2022 posted by hannah

back to blog homepage

it was febaury 2022 when we decided tha we would take a trip down to london after being left at andover train staion, where we tried to call the guard to get us on the train but completely engored us, so we spoke to the guard on the next train, who offered us 2 free return tickets to anywhere on the SWR network.

when it was the day of our trip to london we decided, we would visit the london sealife aquarium, but unfortunately, it was fully booked for the day and were no tickets avalible on the day. Instead we went into the Shrek adventures attraction as my mum wanted to go in last time but never got the chance, luckly they had some tickets left at the time we wanted.

So as we went in, we enter a magical lift which took us up a floor had the chance for a toilet break (inside the toilets they had speakers wwhich said stuff like "ewww you've washed your hands" in shrek's voice) and then got our photo taken while we are waiting to go in the main attraction, after waiting, we were in this big room where one of the staff at the attraction explained through some legal/ health and saftey proticols, so if anything bad was to happen then we would know what to do before entering the main attraction bus ride.

(unfortunately i wasn't allowed to take pictures of the main attraction so i'll describe it instead) so the bus takes you on a magical bus jurney from london to the far far away land which was driven by donkey however we accidently stood of rumple stiltskins fravourite wicked whitch, we make us in a lot of trouble, so we were task to find 3 ingredients to make a potion that would take us home (back to london), however we had to do it quickly before rumple stiltskins got his hand on us. after going through different parts of the attraction we all worked as a team to get the ingredients to give the baker who used his cauldron to blend them altogether.

after blending together an portal opened up and we were asked to say some magic words to keep the portal open, however, rumple stiltskins had sabotaged the portal, so intead of being transported back to london/home we were transported to a old jail where we were traped. luckly we had the help of shrek to help us get set free and we finaly made it back to london/ home safely. it was really fun and would definitely recomend it to anyone who is thinking of going.

after we finished in the attraction and the giftshop/ receving our photos we decided we would go shopping at westfeld (sheperd bush) shopping center. was going to go by the underground but was appalled to find out that not all stations on the london underground are wheelchair accessable/ have lift access to get down to the platform. so we had to go by bus instead, I followed the TFL staff members directions wrong so we went in a complete circle.

after going in a circle we finally went the right way, (on the way i had to stop as I found a restruant named after me) then we finally found the bus stop. the bus we wanted arrived however when it arrived it was so packed we couldnt get on eventhough they are ment to give up the wheelchair spot when a wheelchair person wants to get on. the bus driver did apologise, said there was another one in 4 minutes, 30-45 minutes later another bus turned up which we managed to get on, It was definitely strange as with buses from my area you all get on from the front, however in london their buses have 2 doors and a wheelchair user would get on the middle door instead of the front door.

an hour and a half later we arrive near westfeld, however we goes in w12 instead thinking this was the main shopping center, it wasnt and didnt reliase until going in and around w12 to then see westfeld over the other side of the road. when we arrive and had a look around westfeld it was big and had a lot of shops, including a primark and jd sports where i picked these pink high-top sb-dunks up

after shopping we got back on the bus to go back to waterloo station to get the train home, I can say we definitely had a great day and would go again, (which I do in 3 months time when I ride the purple train aka Elizabeth line, check back soon for that blog post)
